RULES OF ENGAGEMENT

  • No Jumping: You must complete the phases in order. The difficulty ramps up, so you can't just skip ahead.
  • Team Sizes: You can play solo or in a team of up to exactly 3 members. Manage your team in the Account page!
  • Respect the infrastructure: Do not launch DDoS attacks or use automated noisy scanners that overwhelm our servers. The target is logic, not bandwidth.
  • No player versus player: Collaboration is encouraged, but actively messing with other participants is grounds for an immediate ban.
  • Leaderboard: When you join a team, your individual ranking is hidden and you appear under your team only — even if your solo rank was higher. If you leave the team later, your individual ranking will be restored.
  • Prize Eligibility: Each contestant is eligible for a phase prize only once. If you win a phase prize as a solo player and later complete the same phase with a team, you won't receive the prize again (and vice versa). Your teammates can still win if they haven't claimed that prize before.
